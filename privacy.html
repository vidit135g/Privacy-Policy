Permissions for Replify Messaging App

    Core Permissions:
  • android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
  • android.permission.CALL_PHONE: For directly making calls from the SMS details section.
  • android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE: For starting foreground service for backup/restore on Android P+.
  • android.permission.INTERNET: For sending and receiving MMS if the internet is used.
  • android.permission.READ_CONTACTS: To load contacts to the app for viewing SMS by name.
  • android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE: To check the current state of the phone.
  • android.permission.READ_SMS: For reading and displaying SMS.
  • android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
  • android.permission.RECEIVE_MMS: For receiving MMS from recipients.
  • android.permission.RECEIVE_SMS: For receiving SMS.
  • android.permission.SEND_SMS: For sending SMS.
  • android.permission.VIBRATE
  • android.permission.WAKE_LOCK
  • android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: For creating backups and storing them in internal storage.
  • android.permission.WRITE_SMS: For creating SMS services.

The permissions specifically used in the Replify text messenger are core permissions, ensuring that the information used remains on your device.
